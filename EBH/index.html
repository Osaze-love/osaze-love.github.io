Meet the founder

Freda Anyanwu is a serial entrepreneur with multiple businesses in the hospitality industry. Her passion for entrepreneurship and people development led to the birth of EBH Africa, a social enterprise to empower entrepreneurs with the right knowledge, skills and experience to build and grow a successful business. She also serves as the Project Lead for the Edo Future500 initiative of the Edo State Skills Development Agency that is focused on enterprise development in Edo State. She is the 2021 awardee of the Super woman under 30 Awards, 2021 young entrepreneur of the year award by the Entrepreneur Africa awards alongside other many awards, nominations and features.
