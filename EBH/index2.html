About us

E-Business Hub (EBH) Africa is a social enterprise organization that is focused on entrepreneurship development in Africa. Our objective is to empower young people with the skills, mindset and experiences to build a globally successful business. Beyond entrepreneurial skills training, EBH Africa provides a real-world advantage to entrepreneurs through capacity building, business support services, access to a community of like minded entrepreneurs and access to funding opportunities

Our Back Story

EBH Africa started as community for entrepreneurs to foster collaboration and provide a support system for learning and growth to both aspiring and already existing entrepreneurs. Soon after the community was founded, the global pandemic happened causing a lot of businesses to shut down. The community served as a place of support to entrepreneurs at the time and it grew overtime due to the massive support it was providing not just to Nigerian business owners but business owners across other African countries and beyond. Now the community has expanded into providing business development services to African entrepreneurs through its digital hub.

Why Entrepreneurship?

We strongly believe that entrepreneurship plays a major role in economic development. Entrepreneurs represent the demographic with the most potential to create jobs, reduce poverty and generate revenue in Africa's economy. This is why we are focused on building an ecosystem to promote business growth and sustainability in Africa

Mission

To empower African entrepreneurs with the right tools and resources to build and scale sustainable businesses.

Vision

To build an ecosystem for economic development with the right tools and resources to build and scale sustainable businesses

Focus Areas

Business Support

We provide business support to entrepreneurs to help them navigate a successful path.

Capacity Building

We are focused on building the skills, mindset and experience of aspiring and already existing entrepreneurs through trainings, mentorship, events etc.

Community

The EBH Africa community provides entrepreneurs with a global network to foster collaboration, networking and business growth.

Access to Funding

One of the major challenges faced by entrepreneurs in Africa is access to funding. EBH Africa seeks to bridged the gap by providing access to funding opportunities through loans, grants etc.

Recent Projects

Young Entrepreneurship Network

This initiative is designed specially for undergraduates, recently graduated and corp members looking to start or grow their entrepreneurial journey. Through this project, young individuals will be provided guidance on their business ideas, get the right trainings and mentorship to grow a sustainable business.

Women Enterprise Program

This project offers a multidimensional approach to remove the constraints women entrepreneurs face in Africa. Women entrepreneurs are disproportionately represented in the informal economy making them more vulnerable to crisis and less likely to access finance to expand their businesses and productivity.

Idea To Launch

A business incubation program for idea and early stage businesses. The program will select African entrepreneurs in cohorts and provide them with the training, resources and mentorship needed to build a sustainable business. At the end of each cohort, successful entrepreneurs will pitch their ideas to access funding.

Meet the founder

Freda Anyanwu is a serial entrepreneur with multiple businesses in the hospitality industry. Her passion for entrepreneurship and people development led to the birth of EBH Africa, a social enterprise to empower entrepreneurs with the right knowledge, skills and experience to build and grow a successful business. She also serves as the Project Lead for the Edo Future500 initiative of the Edo State Skills Development Agency that is focused on enterprise development in Edo State. She is the 2021 awardee of the Super woman under 30 Awards, 2021 young entrepreneur of the year award by the Entrepreneur Africa awards alongside other many awards, nominations and features.
